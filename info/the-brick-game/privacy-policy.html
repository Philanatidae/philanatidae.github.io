The Brick Game - Privacy Policy

The Brick Game (the "Software") does not store any user-identifiable data. Data is stored locally on-device for the functionality of the Software (settings, save games, etc.).

Data may be collected by the distribution method of the Software; either the Apple App Store (Apple devices), Google Play Store (Android devices), or Amazon Appstore (Android/Windows devices). This data is anonymous and is used for improvement of the Software only. Refer to the licenses and documentation of the distrubition method for more information.

This privacy policy may be updated only to reference additional distribution methods. This policy will be updated prior to being available on an additional distribution method.
